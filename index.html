About me

Nse / Distactor / Hawk

Nse is a rapper, content creator and gamer from VietNam. Nse has been making YouTube videos since 2017 with the name "Shaki Kenito" then changed to Nse since 2020.

Nse started to rap on January 2020 with other alter ego "Distactor" (dark and negative version of Nse) and released the first song called "Freedom".
NF and EMINEM brought Nse to rap so Nse rapped in lyrical style.

Nse took part in Nine Tailed Fox E-Sports and started to play in the Nine Tailed Fox's Valorant Team with the name "Hawk" on 8th March 2021.
